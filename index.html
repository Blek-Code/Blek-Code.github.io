02 / Process

How a project moves through the farm.

Five phases. No theatre. Every week ends with something you can click.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Workshops, interviews, and a written scope you can actually understand.

  2. 02

    Design

    Wireframes to high-fidelity. We test on real users, not opinions.

  3. 03

    Build

    Two-week sprints. Demos every Friday. Code reviews every commit.

  4. 04

    Launch

    Phased rollout, observability dialed in, on-call from day one.

  5. 05

    Support

    Retainers, SLAs, and a team that remembers your codebase next year.
